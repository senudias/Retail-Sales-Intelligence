# Retail Sales Intelligence & Profitability Analysis (SQL)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL to answer real business questions and uncover actionable insights. The objective is to simulate the role of a Business Intelligence/Data Analyst by transforming raw sales data into meaningful business information.

## 🎯 Business Scenario

A retail company wants to understand its sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends to make better business decisions.

As the Data Analyst, my responsibility was to analyze the data and provide insights using SQL.

## 🛠️ Tools Used

## 📂 Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset contains information about:

* Orders
* Customers
* Products
* Categories
* Sales
* Profit
* Regions
* Order Dates
* Shipping Details

## 📊 Business Questions Answered

* What is the total revenue?
* What is the total profit?
* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which region is the most profitable?
* Which product categories perform best?
* Who are the top customers by sales?
* Which customers generate losses?
* Which products generate high sales but low profit?
* How do discounts affect profitability?
* What are the monthly sales and profit trends?
